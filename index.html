About Me

Hello! this is Hao Li (in Chinese 李浩). Welcome to my website.

I am a Lecturer in GIS in the Department of Geography, National Unviersity of Singapore, where I am faculty member of the the GIS Unit and an affiliated member of NUS Artificial Intelligence Institute (NAII).

I hold a PhD dgree with distinction (magna cum laude) in Geoinformatics from the GIScience Research Group at Institude of Geography, Heidelberg University in 2022. Before NUS, I was a Postdoc Research Associate at the Department of Aerospace and Geodesy, Techinical University of Munich (TUM). As a background, I studied Geomatics Engineering (MSc), Cartography and GIS (BSc), and Computer Sceince (BSc), in University of Sttutgart (2015-2018) and Wuhan University (2011-2015), respecively.
